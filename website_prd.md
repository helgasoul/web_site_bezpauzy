## 11. Risks & Mitigation

### 11.1. Technical Risks

**Risk 1: Performance Issues with Large Content Volume**
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: High (bad SEO, poor UX)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Use Next.js ISR (Incremental Static Regeneration)
  - Implement aggressive caching
  - Use CDN (Vercel edge network)
  - Image optimization (WebP, lazy loading)
  - Code splitting
  - Monitor Core Web Vitals weekly

**Risk 2: Supabase Scaling Limitations**
- **Likelihood**: Low (for MVP)
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:
  - Start with Free tier, upgrade to Pro as needed
  - Monitor query performance
  - Implement connection pooling (PgBouncer)
  - Have migration plan to self-hosted PostgreSQL if needed
  - Regular database performance audits

**Risk 3: Security Breach / Data Leak**
- **Likelihood**: Low
- **Impact**: CRITICAL (legal, reputation)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Follow OWASP Top 10 best practices
  - Regular security audits
  - Implement CSP headers
  - Use Supabase RLS properly
  - Encrypt sensitive data (already in place)
  - Have incident response plan
  - Cyber insurance (consider)

### 11.2. Business Risks

**Risk 4: Low Traffic (SEO doesn't work)**
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:
  - Diversify traffic sources early:
    - Paid ads (Yandex Direct) for key articles
    - Influencer partnerships
    - Guest posts on high-DA sites
    - Telegram channel cross-promotion
  - Focus on long-tail keywords (easier to rank)
  - Build email list as owned channel
  - Consider PR (media mentions)

**Risk 5: Low Conversion (traffic doesn't convert to bot users)**
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:
  - A/B test CTAs extensively
  - Improve value proposition messaging
  - Make bot onboarding frictionless (deep links with context)
  - Offer lead magnets (checklists, PDF guides)
  - Retargeting campaigns (Yandex Direct, VK)
  - Exit-intent popups (use sparingly)

**Risk 6: Content Production Bottleneck**
- **Likelihood**: High
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Mitigation**:
  - AI-assisted writing (with doctor review)
  - Hire freelance medical writers
  - Repurpose bot queries into blog content
  - User-generated content (interviews, case studies)
  - Build content buffer (2-4 weeks ahead)

**Risk 7: Book Doesn't Sell**
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: Medium (affects revenue but not core business)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Pre-validate demand (pre-orders before writing full book)
  - Offer generous preview (2 chapters free)
  - Build hype via newsletter (behind-the-scenes)
  - Attractive bundling (book + Paid1 subscription)
  - Partner with influencers for promotion
  - Plan B: Give book free to Paid2 subscribers (value-add)

### 11.3. Legal/Regulatory Risks

**Risk 8: –§–ó-152 Non-Compliance**
- **Likelihood**: Low (if followed properly)
- **Impact**: CRITICAL (fines, shutdown)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Legal review of privacy policy
  - Ensure Supabase data stored in compliant region (EU or Russia)
  - Proper consent flows
  - GDPR/–§–ó-152 training for team
  - Regular compliance audits
  - Have lawyer on retainer

**Risk 9: Medical Liability (giving bad advice)**
- **Likelihood**: Low
- **Impact**: HIGH
- **Mitigation**:
  - Strong disclaimers on every page:
    "–ò–Ω—Ñ–æ—Ä–º–∞—Ü–∏—è –Ω–µ –∑–∞–º–µ–Ω—è–µ—Ç –∫–æ–Ω—Å—É–ª—å—Ç–∞—Ü–∏—é –≤—Ä–∞—á–∞"
  - All content reviewed by licensed doctors
  - Never give diagnostic advice
  - Malpractice insurance (consider)
  - Clear distinction: educational vs. medical advice

**Risk 10: Copyright Issues (images, content)**
- **Likelihood**: Low
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Mitigation**:
  - Use only licensed stock photos (Unsplash, Pexels, paid stock)
  - Create original illustrations
  - Properly attribute scientific sources
  - Don't copy-paste from other sites
  - AI-generated content: human editing + fact-checking
